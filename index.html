Drilling

OPERATION ▾
POSITION ▸
1 hole
PARAMETERS ▸
CYCLE ▸

Auto layered: full retract every 1×D (G00 to clearance R, re-enter 1mm above), small every ½D (G00 back 2mm, re-enter 0.5mm at 2× feed, continue), micro every ¼ or ⅛D. Full needs no through-coolant. Cells editable when an Advanced mode is on.

CUSTOMISATION ▸
ADVANCED — DRILLING ▸

Zone = previous end to its End depth (mm). P1 = peck/drill dist, P2 = retract/back-off (units above). Plunge: no retract. Full retract: peck P1, rapid fully out. Partial: drill P1, back off P2. Chip break: peck P1, tiny back P2.

Layered retract by counts: Full > Small > Micro. Degressive shrinks all three together (keeps full:small:micro ratio) by the reduction % each full cycle, until the limit; then the peck size holds constant to the bottom.

PARAMETERS ▸

Vc/RPM from material (threading values). Rough DOC = 2×nose R, rough feed = nose R÷4. Finish DOC = nose R÷2, finish feed = nose R÷8.

POSITIONING ▸

Safe 1 = home. Safe 2 = roughing clearance: tool goes here before cutting; between passes it retracts only to Safe 2 X (default 2 mm off raw dia). Safe 2 Z default = raw face + 2.

Z bounds limit how far along the part roughing runs. Default: raw face → deepest profile Z.

CUT SETUP ▸

Stepped = constant-X passes ∥ Z. Copying = follow profile, offset by stock. Roughing = multiple passes; Finishing = single pass.
